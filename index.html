Introduction

My name is Noah. I am an 18-year-old Dutch student who is studying the bachelor Creative Technology at the VU/University of Twente. In this bachelor I work together with a team of fellow students to find creative technological solutions to broad problems for clients and/or educational purposes. Before I started this bachelor, I’ve completed the 6-year program called “The Coderclass” at Metis Montessori Lyceum Amsterdam. In this course I have learned to code in many different languages and learned about techniques such as object-oriented programming. I’ve also learned a lot about teamwork and collaboration in a semi professional environment using software such as git in my time in the Coderclass. In my free time I also prototype products and services from a list of ideas that I expend on every once in while. On this page you can find a collection of school projects and prototypes of products I made in my free time.

WFC map generation

July 2022 - August 2023 (highschool)

This was a project inspired by a youtube video of someone doing 3d map generation with the Wave Function Collapse algorithm. This video made me want to try and make a little simpler version. In this version, you have deep water (dark blue), water (blue), sand (yellow), grass (green), three (brown), and a big three (dark brown). They each have rules such as deep water can't be near a non-water title, with the wave function collapse Algorithm I enforce these rules and generate a random map.
