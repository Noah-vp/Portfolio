WFC map generation

July 2022 - August 2023

This was a project inspired by a youtube video of someone doing 3d map generation with the Wave Function Collapse algorithm. This video made me want to try and make a little simpler version. In this version, you have deep water (dark blue), water (blue), sand (yellow), grass (green), three (brown), and a big three (dark brown). They each have rules such as deep water can't be near a non-water title, with the wave function collapse Algorithm I enforce these rules and generate a random map.
